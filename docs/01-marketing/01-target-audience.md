# Target Audience

We consider the target audience of the service to be:

- People who want to adopt a stray cat or dog.

## Assumed user profile for those providing shelter services through the service

- Men and women between the ages of 18 and 65.
- Have a secondary or higher education.
- Able to use a computer to post listings on the service.
- Love animals.

## Assumed user profile for those using the service

- Men and women between the ages of 18 and 65.
- Education is not important.
- Able to use a computer to search for listings and communicate with the poster.
- Love animals.
